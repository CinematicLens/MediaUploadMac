Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MediaUploadCloud handles your data.

1. What the app does

MediaUploadCloud exports media on your device and can upload files to cloud providers you configure.

Important: Upload/data transfer is performed from the Queue workflow you control.

2. Data processing

  • Media export/transcoding is performed locally on your Mac.
  • You choose where files are uploaded (S3, Azure, GCS, Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, or HTTPS endpoint).
  • The app does not sell your personal data.

3. Credentials and tokens

  • Cloud credentials/tokens are used only to perform uploads you initiate.
  • Where supported, secure storage paths such as Keychain are used.

4. Logs and diagnostics

  • Operational logs may include file/job metadata needed for transfer troubleshooting.
  • You can copy, save, and clear logs from the app UI.

5. Third-party cloud services disclaimer

We provide limited support from our side for upload configuration and app behavior.

Important: We are not responsible for uptime, incidents, policy changes, API behavior, or full support of third-party cloud services (including S3-compatible providers). Due to complex external infrastructure, we cannot guarantee 100% functionality at all times.

6. Your control

  • You control destination paths, providers, and upload actions.
  • You can remove jobs and clear saved credentials from the app.
